About me

After studying marketing and advertising, and having spent few years working as a waiter, in mid-2018 I started the 20H Talent bootcamp.
Although I did not have any notions about programming, I needed a change in my life and decided to bet on this professional path of which my brother had spoken to me quite well.

After several months of intense study and work, I achieved the goal: to have the necessary knowledge to be a front-end developer.

Every day I try to improve my way of writing code, trying to do it cleanly, easy to understand and follow good practices.
